Attempt

Our project is an attempt to build a website for cricket fans catering to their needs of extensive statistics, ranking, news and matches.


Features

Our website can show details about live, upcoming and recently concluded matches. A separate section for women's cricket has also been provided. You can also create an account, build a small profile and search for cricket records. Searched records can be found in favourites. Latest news and plethora of statistics can be found on our website. The website is as mobile responsive as possible.
